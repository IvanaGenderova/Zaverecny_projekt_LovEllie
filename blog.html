2. Think of Other Days of the week or month.

Choosing a mid-weekday or off-peak month can affect the budget, as you may sometimes avail of discounts.

3. Go Large.

Consider a single large menu as well as a seating chart that can be placed at the reception instead of printing individual cards. You can purchase a downloadable customizable menu template for printing at your local printer.

4. Greenery as an alternative to flowers.

Choose bouquets made from eucalyptus or any greenery that is in the season during your wedding.

5. Rent a wedding dress instead of buying a new one.

If purchasing a wedding dress for one-time use only that will be additionally taking up the space in your wardrobe does not make you feel right, consider renting it.
